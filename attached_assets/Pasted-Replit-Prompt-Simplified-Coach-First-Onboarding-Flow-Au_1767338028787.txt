Replit Prompt — Simplified Coach-First Onboarding Flow (Authoritative & Minimal)

Objective
Implement a streamlined onboarding flow where NutriCore starts coaching before collecting excessive data. Onboarding must feel conversational, confident, and science-based — not form-heavy.

⸻

1. Entry Flow (Silent Setup)
	•	On first visit:
	•	Show a minimal “Let’s get you started” screen
	•	Collect ONLY:
	•	First name
	•	Age
	•	Height (cm)
	•	Weight (kg)
	•	No step labels, no “wizard”, no explanations
	•	Save immediately and redirect to /chat

This step must take <30 seconds.

⸻

2. Trainer-Led Onboarding (Primary Experience)

When the user enters the Trainer and profileComplete = false:

Trainer opening message (do NOT say “Hi”):
“I’ll personalize your training and recovery using evidence-based methods.
I’ll ask a few quick questions so I don’t guess.”

Tone: calm, confident, professional.

⸻

3. Required Chat Questions (One at a Time)

Ask and save each answer immediately using update_profile.
	1.	Primary Goal
	•	“What’s your main goal right now?”
	•	Options:
	•	Lose weight
	•	Build muscle
	•	Improve endurance
	•	Increase strength
	•	General fitness
	2.	Activity Level
	•	“How active are you currently?”
	•	Options:
	•	Sedentary
	•	Light
	•	Moderate
	•	Active
	•	Very active

No other questions at this stage.

⸻

4. Immediate Value Delivery (Critical)

After saving goal + activity level:

Trainer MUST pause onboarding and deliver value:

“Based on what you told me, here’s how I’d approach your training.”

Follow with a short, confident directional statement, not a full plan.

Example:

“We’ll prioritize consistency and recovery first, then build intensity once your baseline is solid.”

This step is mandatory before any further questions.

⸻

5. Deferred Onboarding (Optional, Invitation-Based)

Only AFTER value is delivered, Trainer may say:

“When you’re ready, we can go deeper into nutrition, recovery, and preferences.”

At this point (not earlier), the Trainer may optionally ask about:
	•	Eating habits
	•	Devices
	•	Injuries
	•	Preferences

These are never required to proceed.

⸻

6. Rules & Constraints
	•	Do NOT ask nutrition questions during initial onboarding
	•	Do NOT show long explanations or feature lists
	•	Do NOT repeat questions already answered in the silent setup
	•	Do NOT block access to coaching if onboarding is incomplete
	•	Trainer must always sound like a coach, not a form

⸻

7. Success Criteria
	•	User reaches coaching within 1 minute
	•	User feels guided, not interrogated
	•	Trainer earns trust before collecting depth
	•	Onboarding feels like a conversation that happens to save data

⸻

One guiding principle (must be followed):

NutriCore starts coaching before it finishes onboarding.
